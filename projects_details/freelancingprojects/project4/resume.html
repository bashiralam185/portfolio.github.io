Position: Raising Achievement Assistant/Finance Teacher

Wanting to strengthen my skillset further, I adopted a position working for a local secondary school as a teaching assistant. I exceeded my responsibility; developing and delivering three modules of Finance materials and lesson delivery in my first two terms. This saw me publish and distribute three exercise/textbooks with 50,000 words of content for students, alongside the implementation of a dedicated syllabus which was taught by the Head, Deputy Head, and a Member of SLT, with my work distributed to numerous other schools around Norfolk. By the end of my time teaching at Sewell Park, both years 10 & 11 studied Finance, with an A/A* pass rate twice that of the national average. The results of the Finance course were so strong, that it prevented the school entering special measures, protecting valuable funding and safeguarding jobs. It is an achievement for which I am incredibly proud. Aside from this, I also taught maths, supported extra-curricular activities, took responsibility for a house form group, and organised yearly sailing trips around the English coast for a group of our disadvantaged students through the Cirdan Sailing Trust. The school has also invited me back several times since I left to attend University to assist with safeguarding provisions and for additional Finance support. I was fortunate enough to teach some very tinspirational young people and is a vocation which I would love to one day reunite with. .
